**My honest priority ordering for your specific path:**

The ⭐ questions are the ones I'd tackle first — they're either the most frequent in actual DE/SWE screens, or they anchor a pattern family that unlocks 5–10 other problems once you internalize them. `Merge K Sorted Lists`, `LRU Cache`, `Course Schedule`, `Meeting Rooms II`, and `Find Median from Data Stream` are the five I'd make sure you can solve cold before any interview.

The Hard ones worth grinding even though they're labeled Hard: `Minimum Window Substring`, `Binary Tree Maximum Path Sum`, and `Edit Distance`. These appear frequently enough that avoiding them because of the label would be a mistake.
